Yes. The Diamond Package is available as a standalone marketing system. You do not need to implement the AI CRM to benefit from it though combining both creates a significantly more powerful patient acquisition and retention engine.

Google Ads typically generates leads within the first week of launch. SEO results build over 60–90 days and compound. Most practices see their first new Diamond-sourced patients within 2–3 weeks of going live.

No. Month-to-month after the build is complete. Our clients stay because the results are undeniable not because they are locked into a contract. No commitment required.
