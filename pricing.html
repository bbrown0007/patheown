FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Is Patheown truly HIPAA compliant?
Yes, completely. We sign a Business Associate Agreement before any data is discussed or any system is built. Every tool uses end to end encryption, PHI-safe data handling, and operates fully within HIPAA guidelines from day one.
Does it integrate with my existing EHR?
Yes. Patheown integrates with most major EHR and practice management systems. There is no rip-and-replace required. We work with what you already have in place.
What is the Fax API Integration?
Our Fax API integration automatically receives inbound faxes referrals, authorizations, clinical documents and routes them directly into your CRM and EHR without manual intervention. No more staff manually checking fax machines.
How does the Form Auto-Fill work?
Using data already in your CRM and EHR, our AI pre-populates patient intake and clinical forms before appointments. Patients only verify, not fill from scratch cutting intake time by up to 80% and reducing errors significantly.
What is included in the Diamond Package?
The Diamond Package, included with Scale, adds a custom conversion optimized landing page, full Google Ads management, and a data engineering pipeline that unifies your EHR, CRM, and ad performance into one real time dashboard built to flood your practice with qualified leads.
Is there a long term contract?
No. There are no long term contracts. Our clients stay because the results speak for themselves. Month-to-month billing after the initial setup is complete.
Not sure which tier fits my practice?
Book a free strategy call. We will audit your workflows, identify your biggest revenue leaks, and recommend the exact tier for your practice with no pressure and no commitment required.
Can I upgrade tiers as my practice grows?
Absolutely. Many practices start on Startup or Growth, see the ROI, then move to Scale. Upgrades are seamless we expand your system with zero disruption to your existing workflows or patient experience.
